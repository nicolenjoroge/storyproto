REA Story
About REA About BPM

Real efficiency,
automated.

REA is the bank's push to automate the repetitive, the manual, and the slow — turning multi-step processes into something that happens in the background, so people can spend their time on the work that actually needs a person.

Meet BPM —
the team behind REA.

Every automation on this site started with someone in Business Process Management asking "why are we still doing this by hand?" Here's a first look at the people turning that question into REA.

AI
Harnessing machine learning and large language models to automate decisions, surface insights, and power intelligent assistants like EVA.
RPA / PowerApps
Robotic process automation and low-code apps that eliminate repetitive manual tasks and put powerful tools directly in the hands of staff.
IBPS
Intelligent Business Process Solutions that orchestrate end-to-end workflows across systems, reducing handoffs and speeding up approvals.
DocuSign
Digital signature workflows that replace paper-based signing, cutting contract turnaround from days to minutes across the bank.
